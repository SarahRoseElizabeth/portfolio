A little bit about me.

Back when I was a shy, big-haired 5 year old I knew that I wanted to do something creative with my life. I remember in elementary school the teacher would ask you to write down what it is that you wanted to be when you grew up and every time I would say “I want to be an artist.” Granted, I didn’t really know what that meant at the time but my answer was always consistent.

Fuelled by chai tea, dark chocolate and Florence + The Machine I pursued this creative impulse and graduated from both the University of Manitoba with an Honours B.A. degree and Emily Carr University of Art & Design with a certificate in Interaction Design Essentials. Through this process and outside of it I feel for the world of design and I fell hard.

With a real natural commitment to the work and a constant desire to learn, I love bringing my pen to paper ideas to life whether it be through Illustrator or with strings of code. As such, I enjoy taking on roles both as a visual designer and/or front-end developer.

In my spare time you can find me binging new sci-fi or historical drama on Netflix, practicing yoga, exploring new galleries or browsing through the city’s best tea shops.
